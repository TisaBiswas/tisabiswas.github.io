Hi, I am Tisa.
My pronouns are she/her/hers.

I am a postdoc in High Energy Physiscs at Indian Institute of Technology in Kanpur, India. I am passionate about inclusive and welcoming teaching and mentorship, and increasing diversity in physics, quantum field theory, and particle physics phenomenology.

Previously, I pursued my PhD in High Energy Physics at the University of Calcutta, in the city of Kolkata where I live and love.

As a researcher, I am involved in looking for new physics phenomena. My focus is to study and interpret the unexplained data, predict what the data could signify from a particular theoretical framework through computational simulations, and refining the theoretical models to better reflect the observed data.
